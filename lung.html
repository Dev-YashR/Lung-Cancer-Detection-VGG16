Inside the
VGG16 Model

The architecture consists of 13 convolutional layers organized in 5 blocks with max-pooling, followed by 3 fully-connected layers. Our custom head replaces the ImageNet classifier with a 4-class softmax tuned on thoracic CT scans.

  • 5 Convolutional Blocks — 3×3 filters, ReLU activation
  • Max-Pooling after each block for spatial reduction
  • Global Average Pooling + Dropout (0.5) for regularization
  • Custom Dense head: 512 → 256 → 4 (Softmax)
  • Adam optimizer with ReduceLROnPlateau scheduling
  • Data augmentation: rotation, zoom, horizontal flip

Health Advisory

Prevention is the most powerful medicine. Know the risks, protect your lungs.

🚬

Stop Smoking

Smoking is responsible for 85% of all lung cancer cases. Quitting at any age dramatically reduces risk. Every cigarette damages lung tissue — stopping now begins recovery within weeks.

HIGH RISK FACTOR
🏭

Avoid Air Pollution

Long-term exposure to PM2.5 particles, vehicle exhaust, and industrial fumes increases lung cancer risk by up to 30%. Use N95 masks in polluted areas and use air purifiers indoors.

ENVIRONMENTAL RISK
🥗

Nutrition & Diet

A diet rich in antioxidants — leafy greens, berries, cruciferous vegetables — supports lung cellular repair. Vitamins C, D, and E are linked to reduced cancer risk. Avoid processed foods and alcohol excess.

PROTECTIVE FACTOR
🏃

Regular Exercise

30 minutes of moderate aerobic activity 5 days a week improves lung capacity and immune function. Active individuals have a 20–30% lower overall cancer risk compared to sedentary lifestyles.

RECOMMENDED DAILY
🏠

Test for Radon Gas

Radon is the 2nd leading cause of lung cancer — a colorless, odorless radioactive gas found in soil. Test your home with an affordable radon kit. Mitigation systems can reduce indoor levels by 99%.

HIDDEN DANGER
🫁

Annual CT Screening

If you are aged 50–80 with a 20+ pack-year smoking history, annual low-dose CT screening is recommended by major health bodies. Pulmo-Scan AI supplements — not replaces — clinical screening programs.
